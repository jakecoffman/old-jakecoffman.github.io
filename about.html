I'm a software developer in St. Louis with 9 year's experience in native application development, game development, web development, and bioinformatics. I have developed enterprise software and hacked at a startup. I have worked in C++, Python, Java, Ruby, Groovy, and Go.

Being a great software developer is more than just knowledge of programming. It's also having the business sense to know when to refactor and when to ship. Developers should crave change and seek out learning experiences. They should be good leaders not because they tell people what to do but because they are in the trenches too, setting the pattern of quality work.

Most of all I love writing software.
